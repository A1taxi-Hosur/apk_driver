Notification Sound for Ride Requests

To add a notification sound:
1. Find or create a short notification sound (MP3 format, 1-3 seconds)
2. Name it "notification.mp3"
3. Place it in this directory: assets/sounds/notification.mp3

Recommended sound characteristics:
- Format: MP3
- Duration: 1-3 seconds
- Volume: Medium to high
- Type: Pleasant alert sound (bell, chime, or notification tone)

Free sound resources:
- https://freesound.org/
- https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/notification/
- https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/notification/

For now, the app will use a fallback beep sound generated programmatically if the MP3 file is not present.
